Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is Orobind ?

    Orobind is making it extremely easy for people to be healthy by connecting people to trusted personal coaches through our apps, we make it possible for people to achieve their health goals and create healthier communities.

  2. What will be the role of Orobind?

    So let’s put it this way : Orobind will be taking the onus to enable you to get fit by losing weight and changing your lifestyle.

  3. Do I need to download the app to be able to engage with Orobind?

    If you have an android or an IOS phone, then you should definitely download our app, it will give you much more freedom to track sessions and engage with Orobind.

  4. How many sessions will be there in a month?

    There will be 12 sessions in a month.

  5. What will be the duration of a session?

    Each session will be anywhere between 45 mins to 60 mins. This includes stretching, warm up and cool down as well.

  6. Where will the session take place?

    Sessions can be carried out either at your home or outdoors.

  7. Is it safe to let coaches into my home?

    Yes. We go the extra mile by doing a police verification of all our certified coaches. But, if you still feel uncomfortable do let us know immediately. We will step in.

  8. How much free space do I require?

    150-200 sq. ft of free space would do. Like your drawing room, terrace, apartment basement, parking lot or your garden.

  9. What equipment I need to buy to get started?

    To begin with you don’t need any equipment to get started. Would be sweet if you have a yoga mat and some basic workout apparels like yoga pants, shorts, etc. For the overly enthusiastic ones we recommend buying a kettlebell or a resistance tube.

  10. Where can I get the equipment required?

    You can drop a note to delight@orobind.com and we will deliver your order within a day.

  11. I have some prior injuries, whom shall I discuss them with?

    You would need to clearly mention, all details related to your injury and your current physical condition before starting the sessions. Our coaches are trained on basic injury management.

  12. I am a few months post delivery, will working out be safe for me?

    Well it shouldn’t be an issue, but we still recommend keeping your doctor in the loop.

  13. What if I’m unable to finish my 12 sessions within a month due to unavoidable circumstances?

    No worries, in such cases, you can reschedule your missed sessions for the buffer week. This means that you will have five weeks to finish your 12 sessions.

  1. How many kgs can I lose in 4 weeks?

    It depends on a few factors like your lifestyle and your diet intake, but most importantly depends on how badly you want it. We have seen users lose up to 5kgs as well.

  2. Who will take care of my diet?

    Your diet will be taken care of by our panel of dieticians and a complete diet chart will be provided to you. You also have direct access to the nutritional experts through our chat option or you could even request a call back.

  3. I have never worked out in my life before; will this program be safe?

    Well like they say, there is always a first time. Workout and diet recommendations are completely based on your lifestyle and factors in that you have never worked out before. So expect a lot of hand holding and motivational support to begin with.

  4. How many diet counselling sessions will I get?

    Depends on how curious and enthusiastic you are. We have built in features where you can get nutritional support with just a tap of a button through our app.

  5. How do I stay in touch with my coach?

    You can use our chat feature within the app to stay in touch with your coach.

  6. Are taking supplements safe?

    Well yes, there is ample research available online, but we don’t like to influence your personal beliefs and principles. If you are not comfortable with taking supplements, then that’s how it will be. If someone from our team tries to enforce supplements on to you against your will – do let us know – you won’t find that person again in our organization.

  7. How do I pay Orobind?

    The payment process is very simple – standard online payment modes are available on our app.

  8. Do I need to make the full payment in advance?

    Yes, we operate on a pre-paid model. You can pay as you utilize the sessions as well.

  9. What if I need to reschedule my sessions?

    You can easily reschedule your sessions through the 'Upcoming Sessions' tab available in your app.

  10. What if the coach doesn’t show up?

    Well it will be really embarrassing for us but if the coach doesn’t show up without prior intimation then we will reschedule the session and ensure that this doesn’t happen again.

  11. Will Orobind focus on Kgs loss or inch loss?

    Our key metric to track is user happiness. For some it comes through weight loss for some through inch loss. As long as you are feeling good we are happy. Also, our focus remains on enabling healthy lifestyle and to encourage staying away from crash diets and over training.

  12. Are there any discounts available if I spread the word around?

    We have an extensive referral program going on, through this you can save a substantial amount of money on your next invoice.

  13. What if during my session i need to travel or need to discontinue for some time. Will I lose the balance sessions?

    No. You have the option of pausing your subscription for that period and can resume it when you are able to. If your first coach is available when you are ready to resume, he/she will be assigned, if not available, then a new coach will be assigned.
